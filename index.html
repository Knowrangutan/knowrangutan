Rules (Please read it if it's the first time you play (Click)) :
  • Guess the mystery country in 6 tries. Each wrong guess unlocks a new clue, difficulty is high, so don't worry if you don't find it.
  • Answers must be the country's common english name, lowercase, spaces only, no hyphens, no extra spaces (e.g. "united kingdom", not "united-kingdom"), spelled exactly right.
  • Territories without their own UN seat (e.g. Greenland, New Caledonia) are counted under their parent country's data.
  • Data from 2023, Knowrangutan stays neutral on political or territorial matters (UN sources).
